Born in Singapore, raised in Mumbai, an undergraduate alumna of LASALLE College of the Arts, and a postgraduate alumna of the London Film School, Natashja has not only lived in and understood the culture and work ethos of the East and West but also has an extensive network of creative collaborators that spans across the globe.

As a Filmmaker, Natashja has crewed on Feature Films, TV Shows and Commercials. She has directed and produced a number of Short Films and Documentaries that have travelled to festivals far and wide. Throughout her career, Natashja has consulted, created content and worked with brands such as LVMH, Roseate Hotels & Resorts, Mediacorp, National Geographic, Condé Nast and more. In addition, she has also volunteered for the UN, Plan International and Isha Foundation.

Although formally a Filmmaker and adept at the various aspects of Production (long and short formats), Festivals, International Markets, Distribution & Exhibition, Natashja started off at a young age with a background in Theatre assisting on several Acting Workshops and Stage Productions. She has produced two plays in India, one of which is a winner of a National award.

Natashja’s interests vary across the creative spectrum to Social Art, Sustainable Fashion, Architecture, Photography and Mysticism. She is the Director of Dr.Rathod Medical Foundation, which aims to improve Diagnosis, Treatment Protocol and overall conditions of Healthcare in India and is also the Director of Marketing & PR at Sakshi (Regd. NGO 1993) which works for Gender Equality, Responsible Sexuality, Water, Sanitation & Hygiene. Best Doctor for Digestion in Mumbai
